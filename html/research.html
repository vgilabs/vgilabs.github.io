Research.

Visual & General Intelligence

Research Topics

01

Learning Methods for Future AI

We are focusing on learning paradigms that move AI closer to human-like flexibility — continual learning that prevents catastrophic forgetting across tasks, domain adaptation across distributions, generalization from limited data, and revising what a model has learned. The aim is models that handle diverse real-world tasks more efficiently.

# Keywords
Continual Learning ↗ Domain Adaptation ↗ Unsupervised Learning Few-Shot Learning ↗ Out-of-Distribution Detection ↗
02

Generative AI

We are advancing generative models across modalities, including diffusion models for images, avatar generation, image manipulation, and 3D scene reconstruction. The goal is to make complex visual generation tractable for AI.

# Keywords
Avatar Generation ↗ Image Manipulation ↗ 3D Reconstruction ↗ Diffusion Models ↗
03

Multi-Modal AI

We are building systems that integrate signals from multiple modalities such as language, vision, audio, and biosignals to reason jointly about complex inputs. We are also studying reliability concerns such as mitigating hallucination, while making robust predictions and detecting anomalies in real-world settings.

# Keywords
Multi-Modal Fusion Back-Channel Prediction Medical AI · Anomaly Detection ↗ Hallucination
04

Embodied AI and Robotics

We are studying how agents perceive, reason, and act in physical and simulated environments, connecting vision, language, and control. Our focus centers on human vision-language-action (VLA) models, alongside instruction-following navigation and manipulation for real-world robots.

# Keywords
VLA VLN Embodied Reasoning Robot Manipulation
05

Privacy and Safety AI

We are making AI safe to deploy by selectively removing what a model should not retain, erasing specific knowledge, identities, or concepts, and protecting individuals against unauthorized personalization of generative models.

# Keywords
Machine Unlearning ↗ Anti-Personalization ↗ Privacy Protection Knowledge & Concept Erasing ↗
